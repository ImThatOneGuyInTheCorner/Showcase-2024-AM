Description

The Dracorex are known for their unique skull but they are pretty average sized dinosaurs. They spanned from 10-12 feet in length and 4-5 feet in height. The Dracorex weighed about 500 pounds which is about the weight of a full grown grizzly bear.

Behaviors and Survival

The Dracorex were rather aggresive creatures who were extremely territorial. They lived in groups of about 4-5 istead of staying in a big group.Due to their extreme territorial instincts, the Dracorex would often exert dominance by getting into headbutt fights with their sharp, boney skulls. These were mainly the males. They communicated both verbally and with motions with their head.

The Dracorex's skull is extremely boney and pointy resembling the head of a dragon. When and Where

66 million years ago the Dracorex roamed the earth. They existed during the Mesozoic era during the late Cretaceous time period. They lived alongside other dinosaurs such as the Hadrosaur Edmontosaur, Ceratopian Triceratops and Torosaurus, Armored Ankylosaurus, Ornomomithid Struthiomimus, Therapods Troodon, and the T-rex. They tend to live around other herbivorous dinosaurs in places that consist of heavy vegetation such as the woodlands, forests, and swamps. They Dracorex mainly populated what is now considered modern day North America and roamed around Iowa, Montana, and South Dakota in their small groups.

Diet

There is a debate between whether or not the Dracorex were omnivores or herbivores. The truth is, they were omnivores. Although their diet consisted mainly of vegetation rather than meat. They would occasionally eat little animals and bugs such as bugs and lizards. The Dracorex consumes about one fifth of their body weight in food every single day. That is about 100 pounds of food a day.

Predators

The main predators of the Dracorex were raptors and the tyrannosaurus rex. Their skull helped them fight off predators. It came in extremely handy for these dinosaurs. Not only did it help make them intimidating to other dinosaurs but it helped them fight back since their skull is so sharp.
